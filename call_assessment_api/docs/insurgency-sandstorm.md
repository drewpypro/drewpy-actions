## Key Summary

Insurgency: Sandstorm is a tactical FPS developed by New World Interactive and published by Focus Entertainment, both reputable mid‑sized companies with a history in the shooter and AA publishing space. The game employs Easy Anti-Cheat with kernel-level access, which many users consider invasive compared to standard user-mode anti-cheat solutions. Community sentiment on Steam is strongly positive overall, with around 85% positive reviews and a "Very Positive" label, reflecting widespread appreciation for its realistic, high‑intensity gunplay and co‑op/PvP modes despite recurring complaints about performance, bugs, and some balance issues.

## Dimensions

### Identity & Ownership (Score: 9/5)

Insurgency: Sandstorm is developed by New World Interactive and published by Focus Entertainment, both established companies in the tactical shooter and AA publishing space. New World Interactive, a studio with roots in North America and operations in Calgary, Canada, is known for the original Insurgency and Day of Infamy, while Focus Entertainment (formerly Focus Home Interactive), a French publisher, has a long track record of mid‑budget PC and console titles. Together they present as reputable, experienced partners with a history of supporting their games post‑launch through patches and DLC.

**Developer:** New World Interactive  
**Publisher:** Focus Entertainment  
**Country:** Canada

---

### Anti-Cheat & Client Risk (Score: 4/5)

On PC, Insurgency: Sandstorm uses Easy Anti-Cheat, and the Steam store page explicitly notes that it "uses kernel level anti-cheat" and that Easy Anti-Cheat may require manual removal after uninstalling the game. Kernel-level anti-cheat operates with very high system privileges and is generally considered more invasive than standard user-mode solutions, as it can monitor low-level system activity beyond the game process itself.

**Anti-cheat type:** Easy Anti-Cheat (kernel-level)  
**Classification:** invasive

---

### Community Sentiment (Score: 9/5)

Steam label: **Very Positive**  
Positive review %: **85%**

On Steam, Insurgency: Sandstorm currently holds an overall "Very Positive" user review summary, with roughly 85% of more than 120,000–129,000 total reviews being positive. Players frequently praise its intense, tactical gunplay, strong sound design, and satisfying co‑op and PvP modes, while common criticisms focus on performance/optimization issues, occasional bugs, and frustrations with balance or matchmaking. Overall sentiment indicates a well‑liked tactical shooter with a dedicated community that accepts some rough edges in exchange for its distinctive, high‑intensity gameplay.
